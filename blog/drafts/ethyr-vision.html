An enumerated list of what needs to happen for an alpha release of Ethyr.

Focus on:

  1. Sharing transparency
  2. Information filtering
  3. Negotiating the transition from meeting to knowing, and bridging the empathy gap

Alpha is focused primarily on #1.

Why not focus on privacy, content agnosticism, etc? Because that's being handled by Golix. That shouldn't be something we need to focus on, because it should never even be a concern. With Ethyr, privacy isn't something we build, it's something we inherently have.

Fully enumerated list

  • Golix security review
  • Golix crypto review
  • Human-readable address mapping and lookup system
  • QR-code address mapping and lookup
