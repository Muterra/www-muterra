Test single hash (h1?)

Muterra started as an investigation into encrypted social networking, but it was soon clear that "yet another social app" wasn't about to fix fundamental structural problems on the internet as a whole. From that point forward, we've been focused on developing an "encrypted information container network" -- eicnet for short -- to make online information private by default.

We currently offer an eicnet service layer geared towards IoT (think cloud hosting for your internet-connected refrigerator) and implementation assistance.

Though our goal is to be a comprehensive eicnet service provider, Muterra currently offers only a public-beta storage provider, though an identity provider and public access provider are in private alpha. The Muterra SP has a simple HTTP API, and the eicnet framework bootstraps client/server security from an initial handshake. We're happy to walk you through the process of connecting your devices to the eicnet.
