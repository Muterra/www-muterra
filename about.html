Muterra gives individuals total power over their data.

Muterra's Mesh-Made-Muse started as a social network built on privacy, but consensual information control is a much bigger issue than "just another social app", so we refocused the Muse on giving people real and meaningful self-determinacy on the internet as a whole. We use privacy-by-default to address core technical shortcomings of the web. Despite this ambitious goal, Muterra is currently a one-man team.

Nick Badger has an exceptionally eclectic background - ranging from robots to theatre to emergency medecine to cars. He is largely self-taught when it comes to tech, but make no mistake: Nick has a knack for complex systems and is constantly pushing for their improvement.

All inquiries should contact Nick at badg@muterra.io.
